Billy Jack's Shack

Billy Jack's is a casual restaurant/bar that speacializes in craft beer and signature burgers. This is the perfect spot to grab a quick bite to eat and a cold drink!

Notable Menu Items
  • Sticky Nuggs
  • B.L.T Burger
  • Muddy Pig Donut
  • Chicken and Waffles

Magnolia's Taco and Tequila Bar

Magnolia's Taco and Tequila Bar feautures modern Mexican Cuisine in the heart of downtown Harrisonburg. There menu offers a wide range of unique tacos and hand crafted margaritas.

Notable Menu Items
  • Chorizo Mac'n Cheese
  • Birria Tacos
  • Buddy's Nachos
  • Blueberry Basil Margarita

Urgie's Cheesestakes

Urgie's Cheesestakes is voted best cheesestake in the valley! This award winning cheesestake joint serves authentic Philly cheesestake inspired sandwiches and has an extensive draft beer list.

Notable Menu Items
  • Urgie's Philly Special
  • Chicken Cheestake
  • Pizza Steak
  • Whiz Fries

Clementine Cafe

Clementine Cafe focuses on food, music, and art - located in the culinary district of downtown. Stop in for brunch any Saturday or Sunday!

Notable Menu Items
  • Clementine Burger
  • Bistro Steak
  • Fish & Chips
  • Biscuits & Gravy

Bella Luna Wood-Fired Pizza

Bella Luna features hand-made pastas, wood-fired pizza, and artisan cocktails. Chefs follow traditional pizza making methods that begun in Naples, Italy.

Notable Menu Items
  • Brooklyn Pizza
  • Risotto Fritters
  • Pasta Bolognese
  • Tiramisu
